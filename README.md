# AI Girlfriend App Cost Dataset 2026

Version: 2026-07-16

Companion Cost publishes this source-backed dataset for comparing the free start, first paid wall, current price evidence, 30-day planning range, variable-spend triggers, cancellation, refunds, privacy, deletion, and testing status of 11 AI girlfriend and AI companion apps.

## Files

- `ai-girlfriend-app-cost-dataset-2026.csv`: flat 11-row table for spreadsheets and data tools.
- `ai-girlfriend-app-cost-dataset-2026.json`: nested rows, field definitions, methodology, caveats, and 63 linked product-source references.
- `CITATION.cff`: machine-readable citation metadata.
- `LICENSE.md`: reuse terms and third-party-source boundary.
- `metadata.json`: distribution and release metadata.
- `zenodo_metadata.json`: the metadata used for the public Zenodo dataset deposit.
- `checksums.sha256`: SHA-256 checksums for the CSV and JSON files.

## What This Dataset Is For

- Compare what can be tried before payment.
- Identify the first subscription, credit, coin, media, trial, or upgrade boundary.
- Separate a current numeric price signal from checkout-dependent uncertainty.
- Keep cancellation, refund, privacy, deletion, and source freshness attached to price claims.

This is not a recommendation ranking, a final checkout quote, or a claim that every app was purchased and tested.

## Testing Boundary

Paid hands-on test: not completed yet.

RedChat has a separately labeled review-account pricing and policy-route check. That is not a paid checkout test.

## Method Summary

Rows use linked public product, pricing, help, terms, privacy, app-store, and policy sources. Affiliate-program-only pages are excluded from the 63-source product registry. Planning ranges are editorial estimates for comparison and can differ from checkout because of region, tax, platform billing, annual billing, usage, and product changes.
